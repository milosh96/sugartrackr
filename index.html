Features

SugarTrackr is packed with amazing features. Check them below to see how best to use the application.

Record your data

Record your insulin intake and sugar levels before and after the 3 main meals - breakfast, lunch and dinner. Also record your basal insulin before sleep.

Keep track

You can access and analyze all your previously recorded data in list views or in line chart graphs. Filter the data by time periods or meals, sort it and check summaries - minimum, maximum and average values. For the better view graphs, group the data by days, weeks or months.

Share PDF report

Generate beautiful and data-rich pdf files with data for the selected period and 3 graphs - daily, weekly and monthly grouped data.

Add reminders

Never again forget to record any blood sugar levels or insulin intake. Set reminders before and after meals.

Choose sugar level units

Choose between 2 sugar level units - mmol/L and mg/dL.

Backup and restore data

Backup your data and share the backups somewhere safe. In case you lose or change your phone, restore everything easily.
