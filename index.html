This mobile application helps you keep track of your insulin intake and sugar levels before and after meals. With a beautiful and modern design, you'll have access to all your previous recorded data in list views or in line chart graphs. You'll be able to filter you data by time periods or meals, and see summaries for your data - minimum, maximum and average values. Additionaly, you can save and share data-rich pdf files, add reminders to record your data and backup and restore everything..
